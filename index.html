Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed answers to common queries about our premium services

What makes Levi Interiors different from other interior designers? +

Levi Interiors offers a truly premium experience with: 1) International design standards, 2) German-engineered manufacturing, 3) 45-day delivery guarantee with financial compensation, 4) 10-year comprehensive warranty, 5) Senior designer attention on every project, 6) Photorealistic 3D visualization with VR walkthroughs, and 7) White-glove installation and handover service.

What materials do you use for premium interiors? +

We source only certified premium materials: Italian laminate (ABET Laminati), German hardware (Hettich, Blum), Austrian glass, American solid wood, and imported marble/granite. All materials come with manufacturer warranties and are selected for durability, aesthetics, and environmental safety.

How does the 45-day delivery guarantee work? +

Once designs are finalized and materials selected, we guarantee project completion within 45 calendar days. This includes manufacturing, installation, and final finishing. If we exceed this timeline due to our reasons, we provide financial compensation of ₹5,000 per day until completion, as specified in our contract.

Can I visit ongoing projects or showrooms? +

Yes, we have experience centers in all major cities showcasing our premium finishes and materials. We can also arrange visits to select ongoing projects (with client permission) to observe our work quality and processes firsthand. Virtual tours of completed projects are also available.
